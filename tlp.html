Transformational Leadership Program (TLP)

Overview

TLP is an immersive residential leadership learning program intended to re – kindle the inherent potential and motivation in young adults that ignite the seeds of transformation in them. The program is designed to help students grow emotionally, socially & intellectually and build leadership capabilities that are imperative in today’s dynamic world.

Main Themes -

  • - Value Based Leadership
  • - Problem Solving & Decision Making (could also include an element of career decisions/career counseling)
  • - Team Work & Trust Building
  • - Soft Skills & Communication Skills
  • - Personality Development
  • - Self Awareness – Connecting with the Self

Distinguishing Elements/ Features –

  • a. Engaging Dialogues & Discussions
  • c. Adventure Activities & Sports
  • e. Educational Tours
  • g. Inspiring Guest Speakers
  • i. Game Simulation based learning
  • b. Theatre Techniques & Performing Arts
  • d. Community Service
  • f. Motivational Films
  • h. Yoga & Mindfulness Techniques
TLP is available in 2 editions – Middle School Edition (for students of 10 – 14 years) and Senior School Edition (for students of 15 – 17 years)
