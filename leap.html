Leadership Exploration And Awarness Program (LEAP)

Overview

A Leadership & Life Skils Program for Young Adults (Non-Residential)

LEAP need to allow students to reflect, look within and become mindful of their inherit leadership skills and potential. The 3 days intesive program is design to create an awareness of the self, help build essential life skills and make informed carrer choices.

Main Elements -

  • Value Based Leadership & Awarness
  • Soft Skills
  • Carrer Choices & Assessment
LEAP is a 3 day non-residential program conducted for Middle School & Senior School Students
